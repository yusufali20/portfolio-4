Non Doc/Gift Item Service

Items above 200 grams and less than or equal to 3.0 kgs which are not documents or parcels are identified as our Non-Documents (Gift item) delivery service. This is a product servicing our retail clients. To make it more convenient and secured we provide a special and unique packing material along with a unique serial number to identify and categorise the enveloping.
The non-document services are available with multiple options using 3-sized poly-pack maintaining three separate colors. They are as follows:

(A) Yellow Poly:

Poly booking rate BDT 110 / (Office delivery),BDT 120 / (Home delivery) - weight limit from 200 grams upto 1000 grams.




(B) WHITE POLY:

Poly booking rate BDT 150 / (Office delivery),BDT 160 / (Home delivery) - weight limit from 1001 grams upto 2000 grams.




(C)Blue Poly:

Poly booking rate BDT 210 / (Office delivery),BDT 220 / (Home delivery) - weight limit from 2001 grams upto 3000 grams.




Note: All kinds of Fragile, Flammable, Frozen, Liquids, Gold, Currencies, Illegal Products are "STRICTLY PROHIBITED". Before packing the products are placed in these poly-packs, they must be seen and verified by our concerned person.
